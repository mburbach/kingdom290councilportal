Kingdom Opening — Essential Tips

INGOTS & SILVER: Send ingots to the palace immediately. Keep resources low — hunters will keep coming back if they find them on you.

ATTACKS: Expect 24/7 attacks from other kingdoms. Stay defensive initially and keep your shield ready.

PORTALS: Never leave an open portal unattended. Foreign portals are darker and more intense red — spot one? Report it immediately to K290-MAFIA.

CLOSING ENEMY PORTALS: Attack with minimal troops — even 1 guardsman works. Never bring your dragon — it reveals your strength!

AYRIN: Only Ayrin travels to other kingdoms without portals. 

BATTLE GROUP: Teleporting outside your Battle Group permanently cuts ties — plan carefully!

Play smart, play your own game — we’re in this together!
