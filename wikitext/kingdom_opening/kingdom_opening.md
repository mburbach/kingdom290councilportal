MINDSET SHIFT:

From opening day think as a kingdom not just a clan. A strong united kingdom benefits everyone.

INGOTS & SILVER:

Send ingots to the palace immediately. Hunters from other kingdoms target ingots and silver — if they find them on you they’ll keep coming back. Keep resources low and only open supply chests when you need them.

ATTACKS:

Expect attacks 24/7 from other kingdoms — that’s normal. Neighbours don’t follow our ROE. Stay defensive initially. Keep a Shield of Peace ready and hide troops in clan forts when needed.

PORTALS:

Every open portal is a target from opening day including event portals. Never leave yours unattended. Enemy portals open briefly before attacks — report all portal activity immediately to K290-MAFIA.

Foreign portals are recognisable by their slightly different, darker and more intense red colour. If you see a portal of a different colour report it immediately!

K290-MAFIA:

A dedicated group watching for hostile acts and monitoring portals. Report any portal opening from outside the kingdom immediately in K290-MAFIA chat OR Kingdom Chat

New players and entire clans may move in — usually stronger than us but that’s not a bad thing. They strengthen the kingdom and create new opportunities. Don’t fear newcomers — our real threat comes from neighbouring kingdoms not within.
